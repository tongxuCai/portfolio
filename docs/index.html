This page collects some of my best data projects done at Columbia Journalism School's Data Journalism Program. My skills might not be fancy enough, but I do believe in the paramount importance of combining simplicity and comprehensiveness in visual storytelling.

That means to extract our storytellers' subjective narratives, so we could present the information as clearly and objectively as possible. Otherwise, charts could lie and be misused in a worse way than biased stories.
